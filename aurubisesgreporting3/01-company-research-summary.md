## Business Model

Aurubis operates as an integrated multi-metal company with two primary business streams:

1. **Primary Copper Production**: Processing complex metal concentrates from mining partners
2. **Secondary/Recycling**: Processing scrap metals, e-waste, industrial residues, and recycling materials

### Key Products
- Copper cathodes (1+ million tons annually)
- Copper wire rod
- Precious metals (gold, silver, platinum group)
- Industrial metals (nickel, tin, zinc, lead)
- Sulfuric acid (byproduct)

## Key Challenges & Pain Points

### 1. **Volatile Commodity Markets**
- Copper prices fluctuate significantly
- Treatment and refining charges (TC/RC) under pressure
- 14% decline in profit before tax in FY 2024/25

### 2. **Complex Supply Chain Management**
- Multiple raw material sources (concentrates, scrap, e-waste)
- Global logistics across 7 countries
- Quality variability in recycled inputs

### 3. **Regulatory & Sustainability Pressure**
- Carbon neutrality commitment "well before 2050"
- EU environmental regulations
- Copper Mark certification requirements across all sites

### 4. **Operational Complexity**
- Multi-metal extraction from complex materials
- Ramp-up costs for new facilities (Richmond, Pirdop expansion)
- Integration of advanced recycling technologies

### 5. **Digital Transformation Challenges**
- Legacy industrial systems
- Real-time production monitoring needs
- Data integration across global sites

### 6. **Talent & Workforce**
- Specialized metallurgical expertise required
- Competition for digital/tech talent
- Training for new technologies (hydrogen, battery recycling)
